Ingredients:
- 125 g butter
- 100 g chocolate
- 2 eggs
- 1 yoghurt
- 2.4 dcl milk
- 120 g sugar
- 1 vanilla sugar bag
- 1 baking powder bag
- 380 g flour
- 150 g choped chocolate, dried fruits,...

Directions:
Melt butter and chocolate.
Mix eggs, add milk, yoghurt, melted butter and chocolate. In another pot mix dry ingredients: sugar, vanilla, baking powder and flour. While mixing wet ingredients, slowly add dry ingredients. At the end add chocolate or dry fruits that you want inside of muffins.
Bake 25 min at 180 degrees celsius.
